# Sales_Perfomance_Dashboard-Dataset
The dashboard provides a clear picture of how sales are distributed across regions such as India, Malaysia, China, Canada, and the US, while also showing which brands Alpha, Beta, Gamma, or Chi are performing best. It highlights the effectiveness of different sales channels like direct sales, online platforms, partner networks, and telesales, and allows comparisons of customer industries such as banking, retail, telecom, healthcare, and electronics.
